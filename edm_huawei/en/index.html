Dear 2022 Seeds participants,

You’ve put in the work. Now bring it to life!

Did your team win your class’s Tech4Good group project? Or have you continued to work on your project even after the Seeds program?

The Tech4Good Global Competition gives you a chance to pitch your Tech4Good project and take it further than ever. The teams selected as finalists will be offered coaching and the opportunity to share your projects with a panel of internationally renowned judges.

Don’t miss out on your chance to make a change!

Application Period: 15th November to 11th December 2022

Final Competition Day: 5th January 2023

Awards Ceremony: 6th January 2023

Who can apply?

Seeds For The Future 2022 participants who have continued working and made significant progress on their Tech4Good projects.

How to apply?

Complete the online application form and submit requested materials by 11th December 2022
